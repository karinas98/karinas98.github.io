PAC-MAN

Rules

  • Your goal is the make as much points without getting eaten
  • When the ghost catches Pac-Man the game is over.
  • When collecting Power Pellets the Ghost will turn blue and you will have 10 sec to eat the ghosts for extra points
  • Collect all the food to win
  • Food = 1 point
  • Power Pellets = 50 points
  • Scared Ghosts = 200 points
